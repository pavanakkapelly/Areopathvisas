🪜 Visa Process – Germany Step by Step

1
Get APS Certificate FirstIndian students must get APS certificate from APS India in New Delhi before applying. Mandatory!
2
Get University Admission LetterApply to German university and receive Zulassungsbescheid (admission letter).
3
Open German Blocked AccountOpen Sperrkonto with €11,208 via Expatrio, Fintiba or Deutsche Bank. Funds are released monthly.
4
Book Appointment at German ConsulateBook visa appointment at German Consulate or Embassy in Hyderabad, Mumbai, Chennai or Delhi.
5
Submit Visa ApplicationAttend appointment with all original documents. Pay visa fee €75. Interview may be held.
6
National Visa D Approved ✈Receive National Type-D visa. Valid for 3 months to enter Germany and convert to residence permit.
7
Register at Bürgeramt on ArrivalRegister your address at local Bürgeramt within 14 days of arriving in Germany. Mandatory!
8
18-Month Job Seeker Visa After GraduationAfter completing degree apply for 18-month Aufenthaltserlaubnis zur Arbeitssuche to find job in Germany.
